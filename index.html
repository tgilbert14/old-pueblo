Field notes / 01—04

A cinematic composite of a Tucson game day.

The Long Saturday is a personal, noncommercial fan project built from generative imagery. It is not documentary footage of one particular day, and it is not affiliated with the University of Arizona.

The third movement takes place on I’oligam Du’ag, in the Schuk Toak District of the Tohono O’odham Nation. The final venue is currently named Casino Del Sol Stadium.
